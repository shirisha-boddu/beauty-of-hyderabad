Ramoji Film City

It’s a filmmaker’s paradise and holidaymakers’ delight. Located at Hyderabad, Ramoji Film City has been recognized by Guinness World Records as the world’s largest film city. Ramoji Film City as one-of-its-kind endeavour offers comprehensive filmmaking services and film-based tourism. Spread over 2000 acres, the multi-faceted thematic holiday destination plays great host to holiday makers, grand weddings, honeymoons, corporate events, family vacations, school and college excursions/educational tours and adventure. Ramoji Film City hosts several seasonal celebrations with carnivals coinciding with the festive calendar – offering year-round entertainment options.
